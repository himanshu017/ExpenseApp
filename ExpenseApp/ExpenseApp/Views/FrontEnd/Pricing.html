Questions?
How can I pay?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover cards. We don't yet support PayPal.

How long are your contracts?

Proctor subscriptions vary from month-to-month or yearly payments. Upgrade, switch, or cancel your plan at any time.

When will I be charged?

Immediately upon signing up, and then every month, three months, six months, or year, depending on the plan you chose. We offer a 30 day money back guarantee if you aren't happy within 30 days of signing up.

How do I cancel?

You can cancel at any time in the billing section of the dashboard. There are no obligations or contracts.
